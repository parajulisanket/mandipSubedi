Strengthening Engineering from Within

These roles were opportunities to listen, build platforms, and push for systems that protect and empower engineers - especially those working in difficult terrain, with limited support, and against time.

Nepal Geotechnical Society (NGS)

Past President

Growing up in the rugged hills of Rolpa, where resources were scarce but dreams were abundant, I learned early on the value of perseverance and purpose. My early education was shaped by determination as much as by textbooks, and the desire to build something meaningful carried me through my studies in civil engineering across various Tribhuvan University institutions. Years later, that journey culminated in a PhD in Geotechnical Engineering - a milestone not just for me, but for the communities I aimed to serve.
